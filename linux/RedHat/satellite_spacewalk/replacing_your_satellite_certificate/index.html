replacing your satellite certificate

  1. login to the Red Hat Customer Portal - self-service Satellite Certificate generation tool

    https://access.redhat.com/management/distributors/

  2. Generate/update your certificate. further information can be found here

    https://access.redhat.com/site/articles/477863

  3. Download your new certificate

  4. Install/Activate the certificate

  5. Restart the satellite server

[root@server ~]# rhn-satellite-activate   -vvv --rhn-cert=/home/seamusmurray/MySat.xml
